Privacy Policy for SU App

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the AUC Student Union ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the SU App mobile application (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information

  • Name: Your full name from your Google account
  • Email Address: Your email address for account identification
  • Google ID: A unique identifier from your Google account for authentication
  • Phone Number: Your phone number for account verification and locker bookings
  • Student ID: Your student identification number (if provided)
  • Major: Your academic major (if provided)

2. User Content

  • Ratings and Reviews: Ratings, reviews, and comments you submit about professors and courses
  • User Profile Information: Any profile information you choose to provide

3. Usage Data

  • Locker Bookings: Information about locker reservations and locations
  • Points and Rewards: Data related to your participation in the points system

4. Location Data (Approximate)

  • Coarse Location: When you use the campus map feature, we may access your approximate location to show your position on the map
  • Location data is only used on your device and is not sent to our servers
  • You can disable location access in your device settings

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  1. Provide and maintain the App: Authenticate your account and enable app features
  2. Process your requests: Handle locker bookings, ratings, and other services
  3. Improve the App: Monitor app functionality and performance
  4. Communicate with you: Send important information about your account and services
  5. Security: Prevent fraud, implement security measures, and ensure server uptime

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored on secure servers and databases
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data
  • User authentication is handled securely through Google Sign-In
  • Your password is never stored by us—it is managed entirely by Google

Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In: Used for authentication. Your Google account information is subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Google Maps: Used for displaying campus maps. Google's Privacy Policy applies to map data

Data Sharing

We do NOT share your personal information with:

  • Third-party advertisers
  • Data brokers
  • Analytics or tracking companies (we do not use analytics tools)

Your information is only accessed by AUC Student Union administrators for legitimate purposes related to app functionality.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Disable location tracking in your device settings

To exercise these rights, contact us at: sutech@aucegypt.edu

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by AUC students and faculty. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: sutech@aucegypt.edu
